Exploratory Data Analysis in Python

I conducted an exploratory data analysis project on the World Happiness Report dataset from Kaggle. The objective was to analyze happiness levels across countries, explore key socioeconomic factors influencing well-being, and uncover global trends over time. This project involved data cleaning, transformation, and visualization using Pandas and Seaborn. I examined the relationship between happiness (Life Ladder score) and economic indicators such as GDP per capita, social support, and healthy life expectancy. Key insights were extracted using statistical measures and data visualizations, including bar plots, scatter plots, and KDE distributions. The analysis revealed regional disparities in happiness, correlations between wealth and well-being, and fluctuations in life satisfaction over time. These insights can inform policy decisions, business strategies, and social development programs aimed at improving quality of life globally.
